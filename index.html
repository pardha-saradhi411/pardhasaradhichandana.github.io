- About

Engineering the invisible infrastructure.

Four years validating the radio access networks that carry billions of conversations and connections - every day, in every weather, across every band.

I spend my days in the messy, beautiful intersection of protocol stacks, real hardware, and customer expectations. RAN testing isn't about pass/fail - it's about understanding why a UE re-attaches at the cell edge at 3am, or why throughput dips during a specific handover sequence.

Currently at Parallel Wireless in the Radio R&D group, I validate O-RAN Radio Unit (RU) features and RF Conformance (RCT) — M/C/U/S-plane, alarm handling, RU recovery flows, eTilt, IQ playback, ACLR/EVM/SEM/Rx sensitivity — plus cross-domain debug spanning O-RU, BBU, LTE protocol stack, NETCONF, and PTP synchronization. Deployments for Ooredoo, V Mobile, and PNG Mobile networks, backed by Robot Framework + Python automation for multi-UE throughput, endurance, and RSRP-controlled long-run tests.

Before that, four years at Mavenir Systems validating LTE and 5G SA / NSA for Deutsche Telekom (DTAG), Virgin Media O2 (VMO2), NEC, Ooredoo, V Mobile, and PNG networks — Carrier Aggregation up to 1.4 Gbps DL, O-RAN CU/DU/RU integration, and root-cause analysis across the full protocol stack.
